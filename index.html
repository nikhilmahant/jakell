About Dr. Sudheendra

Dedicated to providing compassionate and expert psychiatric care

Dr. Sudheendra completed his MBBS from BIMS, Belgaum and then pursued his post-graduation in Psychiatry from the prestigious National Institute of Mental Health and Neurosciences (NIMHANS), Bengaluru. He has additional specialisation in Addiction Psychiatry (DM in Addiction Psychiatry) from the Centre for Addiction Medicine, NIMHANS.

With over 7 years of clinical experience, Dr. Sudheendra has worked at premier institutions including:

  • Amaha Health
  • NIMHANS, Bengaluru
  • AIIMS, Delhi
  • SDM Medical College, Dharwad

His approach focuses on non-judgmental, empathetic listening and comprehensive evaluation to provide personalized treatment plans.
